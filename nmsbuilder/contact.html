WHO WE ARE

nmsBuilder is developed and maintained by the Bioengineering and Computing Laboratory (BIC) of the Rizzoli Orthopaedic Institute (Bologna, Italy). The key contributors to the project are:

  • Gianluigi Crimi (lead developer)
  • Nicola Vanella (developer, webmaster)
  • Giordano Valente (lead expert)
